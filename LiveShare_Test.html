CAHL: The Columbus Adult Hockey League

The CAHL is a massive league consisting of over 20 different divisional leagues separated by both day of the week and skill level. The leagues are based out of 6 different rinks, making it the largest adult hockey league in the country.

Adult Leagues in Columbus

A League

A league does not exist in Columbus any longer. It used to, but only had ex-professional players, and didn't last long.

B League

B League does still exist in Columbus, but there are few teams. Mostly ex-Division A college players and semi-pro players.

C League

C League is one of the bigger leagues. It is made up of a lot of former varsity high school players, ex-pros who have aged a bit too much for B league, and some newer players who picked up the game very quickly.

For example, my next C league game is on this coming Wednesday.

D League

D League is made up of every player between C league and beginners. It is gigantic.

E League

E League is reserved for actual beginners. It doesn't take long for most players to be moved up to the lowest D league after playing in E league a few sessions.
